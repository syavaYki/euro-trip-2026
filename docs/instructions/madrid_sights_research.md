# Madrid — Sights & Experiences Research
## Euro Trip 2026 — Sep 1–5 (4 nights · 3 full days + Segovia day trip)
### Family of 4: Seva, wife, 2 daughters

> ⚠️ **Critical closure checks before the trip:**
> - **Ermita de San Antonio de la Florida (Goya frescoes + tomb)** — closed for conservation since 17 March 2025, no reopening date confirmed. **Verify at [esmadrid.com](https://www.esmadrid.com/en/tourist-information/ermita-de-san-antonio-de-la-florida) before going.** If reopened by Sep 2026 this becomes one of Madrid's essential sights.
> - **Templo de Debod interior** — exterior always free; interior visits by timed entry only, often closed in summer for cleaning. Verify on [esmadrid.com](https://www.esmadrid.com/en/tourist-information/templo-de-debod).
> - **El Capricho de La Alameda** — only open Sat–Sun + holidays. Closes during heat alerts.
> - **Almudena Cathedral crypt** — separate ticket and hours from the cathedral above; check both.
>
> **Family rules:** No bars / clubs. Heavy emphasis on churches, hidden viewpoints, parks, gelato stops, evening walks. The kids will love peacocks (Retiro/Capricho), the Royal Armoury (Palacio Real), rowboats (Retiro), the Egyptian temple (Debod), and Plaza de España fountains.
