# Zaragoza — Food Research
## Euro Trip 2026 — Sep 1–2 (1 night stopover)
### One dinner + breakfast before Madrid train

> Zaragoza has a strong local food culture — *tapas de ternasco* (Aragonese milk lamb), *migas* (fried breadcrumbs with chorizo), *borrajas* (a local green), and some of the best cured meats in Spain. The pintxos scene on Calle El Temple is modest but decent.
