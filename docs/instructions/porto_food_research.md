## 🥩 Francesinha Restaurants

> Porto's signature dish: a multi-meat open sandwich in a spiced beer-tomato-red wine sauce, topped with a fried egg. The sauce recipe is everything.

## 🍦 GELATO & ICE CREAM (NEW SECTION)

Porto has an excellent gelato scene — a perfect family treat after churches, cemeteries, or long walks.

| Name                    | Why Go                                                                                                                                                                                                        | Location                                       | Price      | Family Rating | Tip                                                                                                                                    |
| ----------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- | ---------- | ------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Gelataria Portuense** | Classic homemade gelato, natural flavours, fresh Portuguese milk. Top picks: chocolate hazelnut, salted caramel.                                                                                              | Rua de Santa Catarina area (central)           | €2–3.50    | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐    | Small, no-frills, very local feel. One of the most consistent gelaterias in the city.                                                  |
| **NIVÀ Gelato**         | Italian roots (founded in the 80s), rich and smooth — dark chocolate ganache, stracciatella, ricotta with fig. Multiple locations including **inside Time Out Market**.                                       | Several locations: Time Out Market, Matosinhos | €2.50–4    | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐    | The ricotta & fig flavour is extraordinary. Get two scoops.                                                                            |
| **Neveiros**            | Porto institution — not just gelato but the city's most creative ice cream parlour. **Pistachio affogato** (pistachio gelato + hot espresso shot) is the star. Also: crepes with gelato, cookies, chocolates. | Rua do Almada, Baixa                           | €3–6       | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐    | Perfect after a morning of churches. Kids get gelato; adults get the affogato. Go mid-morning when it's quieter.                       |
| **Santini**             | Iconic Lisbon gelateria now in Porto — famous for ultra-fresh, natural flavours, especially the **fresh strawberry** (seasonal) and lemon. Simple and perfect.                                                | Rua de Santa Catarina / Foz                    | €2.50–4    | ⭐⭐⭐⭐      | The Foz location has a lovely outdoor terrace — great for after a seafront walk. Strawberry gelato here is world-class when in season. |
| **Cremosi**             | Local favourite, very consistent, silky smooth texture, playful flavours. Multiple locations across the city.                                                                                                 | Multiple locations                             | €2–3.50    | ⭐⭐⭐⭐      | One of the most reliable day-to-day choices. Good for a quick stop on a walking tour.                                                  |
| **DONKO Gelato**        | Opened December 2024 — artisan gelato by a Portuguese-Argentine couple. Very smooth, strong natural flavours of local ingredients.                                                                            | Baixa area                                     | €3–4       | ⭐⭐⭐⭐      | Worth seeking out for something a bit different. The Argentine influence adds some unique flavours.                                    |
| **Cremita**             | Handmade ice cream with some of Porto's most creative flavours — look for seasonal specials.                                                                                                                  | Rua de Cedofeita                               | €2.50–3.50 | ⭐⭐⭐⭐      | The Cedofeita neighbourhood location is in a lovely area to walk and explore.                                                          |

**Best gelato route idea:** After visiting the churches on Rua de Santa Catarina (Chapel of Souls) → stop at Neveiros or Gelataria Portuense → walk to Palácio de Cristal for the peacocks and gardens.
