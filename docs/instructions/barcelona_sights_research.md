## 🗺️ Section 2 — Hidden Gems & Local Tips

*Sources: YouTube Barcelona expat guides, Reddit r/Barcelona, SpottedByLocals, local expat community*

> **Key local insight (from expats):** Barcelona divides into "upper" (above Avinguda Diagonal — quieter, safer, more local) and "lower" (tourist areas, Gothic, Ramblas). Sant Gervasi, Gràcia, and Sarrià are where residents with means actually live. No tourist sights = no tourists = no pickpockets.

## 🚇 Section 7 — Transport Tips

> **T-Casual card:** Buy a 10-trip card at any metro machine — €12 for 10 trips, works on metro, bus, FGC, and tram. Approximately half the price of single tickets. Buy only in metro stations (not on buses). One card = one person (not shareable, unlike Portugal).

> **Funiculars (free with T-Casual):** The Montjuïc funicular from Paral·lel metro is free with T-Casual — use it to access Montjuïc. The FGC Vallvidrera funicular (access to Tibidabo) requires a separate ticket.

> **Pickpocket zones:** Las Ramblas + Gothic Quarter + Boqueria + busy metro stations (L1 Sagrada Família, L3 Drassanes). Keep bags in front, phones in pockets. The upper city (Sant Gervasi, Gràcia, Eixample) is safe at all hours.
