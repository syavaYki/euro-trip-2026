### Getting Around

- **Airport → city:** Train R2N from T2 (~30 min, ~€5); Aerobus (~€6–12); Taxi ~€35–50
- **Metro:** T-Casual card (10 trips ~€12–15) covers metro + bus + tram + funicular from Paral·lel
- **Pickpockets:** Gothic Quarter, Las Ramblas, Sagrada Família exit — bags front-facing always
- **Weather:** 28–30°C, sea ~24°C — fully swimmable

### Key Sights

| Sight | Cost | Tips |
|-------|------|------|
| **Sagrada Família** ⭐⭐ | €26–47 (under 16 free) | Book at sagradafamilia.org — weeks in advance. Nativity Tower ticket. Go 09:00 for stained-glass light |
| **Hospital de Sant Pau** ⭐ | €16 (free Sun 15:00–19:00) | World's largest Art Nouveau complex. Almost no queues |
| **Bunkers del Carmel** ⭐ | Free | Best 360° view in Barcelona. Bus 24 to El Carmel + 15-min walk up |
| **Santa Maria del Mar** | Free | Most beautiful Gothic interior in Barcelona. No queues |
| **Barceloneta Beach** | Free | Go 07:00–09:00 before crowds. La Cova Fumada is 10 min walk |
| **Parc de la Ciutadella** | Free (rowing €6) | Rowboats, Gaudí waterfall, palms |
| **Parc del Laberint d'Horta** | €2.23 (free Wed + Sun) | Oldest garden in Barcelona (1791). Genuine cypress maze |

### Key Restaurants

| Restaurant | Why | Notes |
|-----------|-----|-------|
| **La Cova Fumada** ⭐⭐ | Invented the Bomba. Cash only, no sign. Opens 12:00, often sold out by 13:15 | Weekdays lunch only. Plan beach day around it |
| **Quimet & Quimet** ⭐⭐ | Extraordinary montaditos standing bar. Lunchtime Mon–Sat | Arrive at 12:00 opening — tiny space fills instantly |
| **El Xampanyet** ⭐ | Historic cava bar, legendary anchovies, El Born | Opens 12:00 / 19:00 |
| **Cal Pep** ⭐ | Fresh seafood bar, no menu — chef decides | Sit at bar only. Queue or book table |
| **Carrer de Blai pintxos** | Most fun cheap eating in Barcelona | Go 18:00–20:00. Kids pick their own |
| **Bar del Pla** | El Born, vaulted brick, perfect croquetes | Book or walk in early |
